My Work

A Comparative Study Between MediaPipe with Bidirectional Long Short-Term Memory (BiLSTM) and OpenPose with Long Short -Term Memory (LSTM) for Filipino Sign Language Recognition

This study compares two prominent approaches: MediaPipe integrated with Bidirectional Long Short-Term Memory (BiLSTM) and OpenPose utilizing Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM), specifically tailored for Filipino Sign Language (FSL) recognition. Each method offers distinct advantages in capturing and interpreting gestures, aiming to enhance accuracy and efficiency in real-time sign language communication systems. This comparative analysis aims to uncover insights into their respective strengths and limitations, contributing to the ongoing development of accessible technology for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community.
